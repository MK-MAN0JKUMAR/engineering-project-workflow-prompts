# Prompt 8 — Engineering Learning & Roadmap Review

## Purpose

Use this prompt every 2–4 months, or after completing several major feature branches.

Its objective is to review:

- Your current project
- Current AI engineering trends
- Current testing trends
- Current job market expectations
- Your existing skills
- Your future goals

Then generate a prioritized learning roadmap that directly improves your flagship project and your career.

This prompt prevents random learning and keeps your time invested in the highest-value skills.

## When to Use
```
MASTER
    │
    ├── Several Feature Branches Completed
    │
    ├── AI Ecosystem Changes
```
